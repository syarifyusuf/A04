What We Do

Education

Every child has the right to learn. Education is not a privilege, It is a human right. Education is a powerful tool for breaking the cycle of poverty; supporting child survival, growth, development and well-being; and closing the gap in social inequality.

Building A School

We believe that no child should be forced to study outside, or in a dangerous environment. With access to a good education, children can escape poverty. We're building safe and secure classrooms, so that children can focus on their learning, and their futures.

Supporting Parents

When parents live in poverty, their children suffer. Without a source of income, they are unable to provide vital medicine, food and clean water, or pay school fees. Indonesia has the eighth highest number of child marriages in the world, with one in nine women married before they turned 18 years old. We are here to protect their futures!

Open For Volunteers

Volunteers are expected to become education heroes to spread a positive spirit to improve education in Indonesia. Volunteers will later be spread throughout Indonesia so that the goal of equal distribution of education can be achieved.

About Us

We save children's lives, protect their right to a proper education and give children the opportunity to live healthy, successful lives and reach their full potential.

Our Vision

A world where every child realizes the right to survival, education, protection, development and participation.

Our Mission

To inspire breakthroughs in Indonesian education by helping children achieve immediate and lasting change in their lives and their future.

Our Values

We are guided by our five core values: accountability, ambition, collaboration, creativity and integrity.
